This page walks you through the basics and what to do if something feels off.

## Privacy & Data Use

Guestbook collects information needed to record attendance or visits.

Exactly what’s collected depends on how the organization configured the kiosk.

## What data might be collected

Common examples include:

- Name
- Organization ID or barcode value (when scanning is used)
- Time and date of check-in
- Location/device used for check-in
- Optional fields like reason for visit or department

## Who can access it

Typically, access is limited to staff who manage the program or location you’re checking into.

## How it’s used

Common uses include:

- Attendance tracking
- Visitor logs
- Reporting for program operations

## Retention and corrections

Retention rules are organization-specific.

If you believe your information is incorrect or want to request a correction, contact the staff responsible for the kiosk location.
